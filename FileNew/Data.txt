
CAMPUS TRAINING GUIDELINES
1. Be punctual and prepared for class everyday
2. Adhere to training session and break timings
3. Dress appropriately and adhere to UST dress code policy
4. Ask questions and seek clarifications
5. Show courtesy to the speaker and wait your turn
6. Training premises should be maintained neat and tidy
7. Be mindful of UST property.Vandalism will lead to disciplinary action
8. No food or beverages allowed outside of the designated areas
9. Strictly no casual leaves allowed while in training
10. Access to mobile phones are strictly prohibited in the training rooms
11. Be respectful of your own personal space and of those around you
12. Uphold UST values
